March 2026 — Community Initiative

Setting up an association company for hobbyists & students.

PSP members have been asking how they can introduce SageLion's AI agents to their own interest groups without things getting messy. We are creating a lightweight association company to make that easy—and accountable.

How it works

  • Every PSP member uses their PSP email to register interest. That keeps the participant list verifiable.
  • SageLion provides a curated starter kit: one triage agent, one enrichment workflow, and onboarding checkpoints tailored for hobbyists and student makers.
  • Sessions are run in small circles (6-10 pax) so people actually get hands-on time instead of watching a demo.

Why bother?

Plenty of hobby groups want to automate newsletters, exhibitions, or volunteer logistics but don't know where to start. Students want the same guidance without the enterprise jargon. The association company keeps it informal, but builds enough structure to make sure AI is introduced safely and with PSP oversight.

Next steps

We are drafting the constitution, onboarding the first five PSP mentors, and shortlisting venues near Serangoon Garden and Potong Pasir. If you want your circle included in the pilot, drop us a note at sagelioncap@gmail.com with "Association Company" in the subject line.
