March 2026 — Community Initiative

Setting up a storefront for hobbyists & students.

People have been asking how they can introduce SageLion's AI agents to their own interest groups without wrestling with complex software development lifecycles. To make things easy, we decided to launch a dedicated digital storefront.

How it works

  • We've built a curated library of n8n automation agents. Instead of buying expensive consulting hours, anyone can simply download the raw JSON code.
  • The library includes practical templates—from simple inbox triaging to automated lead enrichment—designed specifically for independent operators, hobbyists, and student makers.
  • You paste the JSON directly into your own n8n canvas, connect your API keys, and you're instantly live.

Why bother?

Plenty of hobby groups and students want to automate newsletters, exhibitions, or volunteer logistics but don't know where to start. They want enterprise-grade architecture, but without the enterprise jargon or the hefty retainer fees. The new storefront democratises this access, providing a clean, plug-and-play solution that lets anyone orchestrate powerful AI workflows instantly.
